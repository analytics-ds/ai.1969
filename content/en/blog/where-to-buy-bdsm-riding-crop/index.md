## How to choose a BDSM crop whip: materials, length and sensations {#criteria}

Before buying a **BDSM riding crop**, it is essential to understand the characteristics that influence the sensations felt. The choice of material, length and tongue shape entirely determines the play experience.

### Materials and their influence on impact

| Material | Sensation | Durability |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| Full-grain leather | Supple, cracking and wrapping impact | Very high (10+ years with care) |
| Faux leather / PU | Light to moderate, drier impact | Medium (2-3 years) |
| Silicone | Stinging, sharp and precise impact | High, easy to clean |
| Carbon fibre | Rigid, dry, very localised impact | Very high |
| Bamboo / wood | Firm, resonant impact | High if varnished |

### Length and handling

**BDSM riding crops** generally measure between 45 cm and 75 cm. Shorter models (45-55 cm) offer more precision and suit confined spaces. Longer models (60-75 cm) allow striking from a distance and produce a more wrapping impact thanks to the lever effect. For a first purchase, a length of 55 to 65 cm is a good balance between manoeuvrability and versatility.

## Care and lifespan of a BDSM riding crop {#maintenance}

Investing in a quality **BDSM riding crop** means maintaining it properly to preserve its properties and ensure impeccable hygiene. Maintenance varies depending on the material used.

### Maintenance methods by material

1. **Genuine leather:** clean after each use with a soft, slightly damp cloth, then apply a leather nourishing balm 2 to 3 times a year. Store away from light and moisture. Well-maintained leather can last over 10 years.
2. **Faux leather and silicone:** clean with mild antibacterial soap and warm water. Dry thoroughly before storage. These materials do not require nourishing treatment but are less durable over time.
3. **Wood and carbon fibre:** wipe with a dry cloth after use. Regularly check wooden models for cracks or splinters. A protective varnish can extend lifespan.

Regardless of the material, it is recommended to store the riding crop flat or hanging to prevent warping. A carrying case also protects the accessory during transport.
